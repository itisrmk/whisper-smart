What it does
01

One key. Talk anywhere.

A single global hotkey works in every app on your Mac: your code editor, a browser field, a chat box. Hold to talk, release to insert the text right where your cursor sits. No window to switch to.

02

Runs on your Mac.

Apple-Silicon-native MLX models, Whisper and Parakeet, transcribe locally, so it works on a plane, in a basement, or anywhere you value privacy. Pick Light for speed or Best for accuracy.

03

Cloud when you want it.

Prefer OpenAI's Whisper API for a specific task? Add your key and switch models in a click. Nothing installs or connects until you deliberately turn it on.

04

Cleaned up automatically.

Light, conservative cleanup fixes punctuation and stray filler after each transcription, so what lands in your document reads like you meant to write it.

05

Every word, logged.

History keeps a searchable record of what you dictated, with latency and success metrics so you can spot which model and settings are pulling their weight.

How it works

Three moves. No menus.

On-device vs cloud

Your voice stays yours.

Whisper Smart runs Apple-Silicon-native MLX models right on your Mac, so by default no audio ever leaves the machine. Want a cloud model? That's a deliberate, per-model choice.
