Whisper Smart

Hold a hotkey, speak, release — your words appear right at the cursor. Fast, private dictation for your Mac.

Download for Mac macOS 14+ · Free & open source

How it works

1

Hold

Press and hold your hotkey — like the right ⌘ key — in any app.

2

Speak

Say what you want to write. A floating bubble shows it's listening.

3

Release

Let go — your transcribed text lands exactly where your cursor is.

Features

Works everywhere

Any text field in any app — email, chat, code editors, even terminals. No integration needed.

Local, fast models

Runs Parakeet and Whisper models locally on Apple Silicon via MLX, with streaming transcription while you speak.

Your choice of engine

Local MLX models, Apple's built-in speech recognition, or cloud transcription — switch anytime.

Hands-free mode

Prefer not to hold a key? Toggle dictation on and let silence detection finish the take for you.

Auto-updates

New releases install themselves in the background, so you're always on the latest version.

Transcript history

Every take is kept locally. Re-insert any past transcription with one click.

Private by design

With local models, your voice never leaves your Mac — audio is transcribed on-device and immediately discarded. No accounts, no tracking, no telemetry. The entire app is open source, so you can verify every line.

Requirements

  • macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later
  • Apple Silicon (M1 or newer) for local MLX models
  • Microphone and Accessibility permissions
